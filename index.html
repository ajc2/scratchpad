Scratchpad

Your own personal notepad where you can write anything you want. Have a lot on your mind? Want to jot down a great idea? Write it here and it will stay here.

The contents of this notepad are not sent to or stored on my server. They are kept in your browser's localStorage, meaning that what you write down is persistent on that browser on that device. If you clear your browser data, these notes will be lost, so be careful!

If for whatever reason I can't be trusted, the complete source code of this page is available on GitHub.
